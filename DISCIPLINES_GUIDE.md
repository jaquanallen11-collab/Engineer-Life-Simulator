### ‚ú® Major/Minor Selection Screen

**When:** Right after Ivy Exam, before university selection

**What You Choose:**
1. **Major** (Primary specialization)
   - Determines your primary career path
   - Sets available companies
   - Defines your main job opportunities
   - Affects starting salary range

2. **Minor** (Secondary specialization)
   - Adds career flexibility
   - 30% chance of minor-related scenarios
   - Expands company options (adds 3 companies)
   - Provides one additional job option

**Selection Interface:**
- Grid layout showing all 12 disciplines
- Each card shows:
  - Discipline emoji and name
  - Average starting salary
  - Description
  - Top 2 skills
  - Key stats
- Can't select same discipline for major and minor
- Confirmation panel shows combined path and expected salary
